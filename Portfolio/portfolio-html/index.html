About

I design for the person who has to explain data to a room that doesn't trust it yet.

My work earns that trust — through clarity, not complexity. Precision at the builder layer produces confidence at the stakeholder layer.

I work best with PMs who can distinguish between a user need and a product hypothesis, and engineers who want to be involved in the problem before the solution is fixed. I've led design across cross-functional teams that included legal and compliance — and I've learned that the clearest design requirement sometimes comes from a compliance constraint, not a user interview.
